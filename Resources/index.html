About Me

HI! I'm Claudio an aspiring developer, currently dedicating my free time to studying coding as I make the shift from a background in customer service and hospitality to a career in web development.

After high school, I initially pursued vocational training to become a medical assistant but decided to change direction and started working at a fitness center. It was there that I encountered a technical issue with one of the computer programs, which led me to discover what I later learned were the developer tools in the Mozilla Firefox browser.

That experience sparked my curiosity and motivated me to research and dive deeper into the world of programming. So far, I've developed skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm excited to keep building on this foundation as I grow in the field.
